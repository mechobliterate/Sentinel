## Overview

Microsoft 365 (formerly Office 365) includes Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams, and other productivity apps. Documents are analyzed for features, and diagnostic data is extensively collected.

**Privacy Concerns:**
- Document content scanned
- Extensive telemetry collection
- Connected experiences share data
- Windows integration exposes usage
- Copilot AI analyzes your content
- Teams meetings and chats monitored
- No end-to-end encryption for documents

---

## Quick Settings Checklist

- [ ] Disable "Connected experiences" in Office apps
- [ ] Turn off "Optional diagnostic data"
- [ ] Disable Copilot data usage
- [ ] Review Teams privacy settings
- [ ] Disable "Improve recommendations"
- [ ] Turn off "Editor" intelligent services
- [ ] Review sharing defaults

---

## Critical Settings (Desktop Apps)

### 1. Privacy Settings in Office Apps

**Path:** File > Account > Account Privacy > Manage Settings

❌ **Turn OFF:**
- "Connected experiences that analyze your content"
- "Connected experiences that download online content"
- "Optional connected experiences"

**Keep ON (for updates):**
- Required service data

### 2. Diagnostic Data

**Path:** File > Options > Trust Center > Trust Center Settings > Privacy Options

**Set to:** "Required diagnostic data only"

### 3. Office Intelligent Services

**Path:** File > Options > General

❌ **Disable:**
- "Enable services" (Office intelligent services)
- "Show LinkedIn features"

---

## Microsoft Teams Privacy

**Path:** Teams > Settings > Privacy

**Disable:**
- ❌ "Suggested replies"
- ❌ "Read receipts"
- ❌ "Typing indicators"
- ❌ "Priority access to notifications"

**Be aware:**
- All chats and meetings can be accessed by organization admin
- Microsoft scans content for security
- No E2E encryption by default

---

## Copilot Privacy (AI Features)

**Microsoft's AI features scan your documents:**
- Copilot in Word, Excel, PowerPoint
- Designer AI
- Editor

**To limit:**
1. Don't use Copilot features for sensitive content
2. Disable where possible in settings
3. Assume Microsoft AI processes all content
4. Check with admin about organizational Copilot policies

---

## What Microsoft Collects

**Always:**
- Document metadata
- Feature usage
- Error reports
- Required diagnostic data

**If optional data enabled:**
- Document content analysis
- Usage patterns across apps
- Productivity metrics

---

## Privacy for Organizations

**Admin controls:**
- Organizations can see all data
- Admins have access to files/emails/chats
- Retention policies may store data long-term
- Assume no privacy from organization

**For personal privacy at work:**
- Don't mix personal and work accounts
- Don't store personal files on work OneDrive
- Use personal devices for personal matters

---

## Alternatives

**For privacy:**
- **LibreOffice** - Open source, local
- **OnlyOffice** - Privacy-focused, E2E encrypted option
- **Cryptpad** - E2E encrypted collaboration

---

## Summary

1. **Disable connected experiences** - In all Office apps
2. **Set diagnostics to "Required only"** - Minimal telemetry
3. **Don't use Copilot** - For sensitive content
4. **Separate work and personal** - Don't mix accounts
5. **Consider alternatives** - LibreOffice for sensitive work
